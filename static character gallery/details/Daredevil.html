Daredevil

Daredevil, also known as Matt Murdock, is a lawyer by day and a vigilante by night. Matt lost his sight in an accident but gained heightened senses that allow him to fight crime in Hell's Kitchen.

  • Universe: Marvel
  • First Appearance: Daredevil #1
  • Abilities: Enhanced senses, martial arts
  • Personality: Determined, moral, resilient
