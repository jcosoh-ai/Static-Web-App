Darth Maul

Darth Maul, a nightbrother of clan Zabrak males on Dathomir, is a Sith apprentice trained by Darth Sidious. Known for his rage and deadly skill with a double-bladed lightsaber, he seeks revenge against the Jedi, Obi-Wan Kenobi who defeated him.

  • Universe: Star Wars
  • First Appearance: The Phantom Menace
  • Abilities: Force powers, lightsaber combat
  • Personality: Vengeful, aggressive, relentless
