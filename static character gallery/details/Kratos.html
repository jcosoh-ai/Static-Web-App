Kratos

Kratos is a Spartan warrior turned god-slayer. Haunted by his past, he seeks redemption while navigating the brutal worlds of Greek and Norse mythology.

  • Universe: God of War
  • First Appearance: God of War (2005)
  • Abilities: Super strength, combat mastery, godly weapons
  • Personality: Stoic, ruthless, protective
