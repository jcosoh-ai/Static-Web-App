Nightwing

Nightwing, also known as Dick Grayson, is the former and original Robin and one of Batman’s most trusted allies. After leaving Gotham, he forged his own identity as a hero protecting Blüdhaven.

  • Universe: DC Comics
  • First Appearance: Detective Comics #38
  • Abilities: Acrobatics, martial arts, leadership
  • Personality: Confident, compassionate, independent
