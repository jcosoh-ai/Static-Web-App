Ultron

Ultron is an artificial intelligence created to protect humanity. However, his twisted logic leads him to believe that humanity itself is the greatest threat to peace, which leeads him to try and exterminate humanity.

  • Universe: Marvel
  • First Appearance: Avengers #54
  • Abilities: Super intelligence, flight, energy blasts
  • Personality: Cold, calculating, nihilistic
